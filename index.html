Work

I am a Research Assistant with Prof. Sneha Kasera in the Spectrum Offenders Project. Primarily, I am working on building scalable crowdsourcing system to automate the process of detecting transmitting transmitters in real time with privacy guarantees for the users participating in the crowdsourcing effort. This system is used for spectrum monitoring, detecting illegal use of spectrum and locating the offenders.

Earlier, I worked in project CARTS (Communication Assisted Road Transportation System) where I have worked on using barometer sensor in mobile phones for low power user activity recognition, road traffic congestion detection and user path estimation.
